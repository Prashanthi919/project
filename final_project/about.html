OUR HISTORY

-1997-

Company was founded by Mr.John inventor, innovator and entrepreneur in 1992 credited with developing the predecessor to online shopping. The idea came about during a stroll with his wife and Labrador when he lamented about their weekly book shopping expedition. This conversation sparked an idea to hook a book to their supermarket to deliver the books. Immediately after the discussion he quickly planned and implemented his idea.

-2002-

In the late 2002, E-store was started to become popular. People began to shop online and in the same year Bookstore expanded its limits and switched to a fully electronic product based firm.

-2010-

The era of internet has begun and people started to depend more on internet than anything else and that brought to a golden opportunity to online stores. Bookstore bacame the second major online book store and was awarded by many highly reputed magazines.

-2017-

Now, Bookstore has became the world wide best #1 ecommerce site and is mostly trusted by commom man, as we provide best books at the lowest cost possible. Bookstore will always be there for it's costumers.
